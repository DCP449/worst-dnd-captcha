Step 1: Choose your class

The DM says "just pick anything, it won't matter." That's a lie.




Step 2: The Encounter

What do you do?

Step 3: Dungeon "Puzzle"

The cube dissolves, revealing a floor of 3x3 tiles labelled with damage types: Fire, Cold, Acid, Lightning, Psychic, Radiant, Necrotic, Thunder, Force.

A glowing inscription reads: "Only those who step on the most commonly resisted damage type shall pass." According to this DM's homebrew logic, which tile is "correct"?
